Arcana Math, Boards & Strategy Notes

Natural chest rarity distribution

RarityProbability

Reward model

Chest EV = fixed Undetermined Fate check + rarity-scaled normal loot Undetermined Fate EV per chest = UF drop chance × UF market value

Upgrade-card rule

Shown: Red → Rainbow Can upgrade: green → blue blue → purple purple → gold/orange gold/orange → red red → rainbow Rule: any chest at or below shown rarity gets +1 rarity

Human strategy

  • Orange/red/rainbow = highest known value.
  • Purple+ respected; do not skip reasonable purple+.
  • Green/blue are low loot, but still UF tickets.
  • 2-card logic is default; 3-card is advanced.
  • Final-tile cycling only when a real recovery target exists.
